PLUS
A game on Android that auto generates levels and gets trickier over time.


HOW TO PLAY
• Eliminate all red tiles to generate the next level
• Clicking a tile will flip the tiles adjacent (tiles that share a side)
• Each level will increase in difficulty by one move
